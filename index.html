Common Questions

We've Heard Every Concern.
Here Are Honest Answers.

I'm worried about leaving my current brokerage — what if they pressure me?
That's the most common reason agents stay somewhere that isn't working for them. Brokers use guilt because it works — but ask yourself: whose career is this? Your broker's? Or yours? We'll walk you through the transition professionally. You don't owe anyone your earning potential.
I don't need more training — I've been in the business for years.
Fair. Coaching at PRG isn't remedial training — it's strategic. Billy's there to help you scale, build systems, and break through whatever ceiling you're currently under. If you don't want it, you don't have to use it. But it's there when you do.
I already get leads from my current brokerage. Will I lose that?
PRG provides leads on a 50/50 split — so you only give up commission when you actually get business from us. If you're already generating your own leads, you keep 100% of those. Most agents end up ahead either way.
What exactly is the $100/month fee for?
Technology and E&O (Errors & Omissions) insurance. That's it. You get a premium CRM, admin support, and full coverage — for less than what most brokerages charge just for the desk. No hidden fees, no surprise invoices.
What's the catch?
There isn't one. We broke away from the traditional model specifically because we were tired of the catches. If something feels unclear, ask Billy directly on the call. He'll give you a straight answer — because that's how we run this place.
